Hobbies & Interests

My hobbies and interests include

Playing and Watching Lacrosse – Growing up in Ghana, I never knew or even heard anything about lacrosse but after seeing it on T.V, It quickly became part of my weekend routine. And in my sophomore year in high school, a friend of mine on the school lacrosse team forced me to give it try and I instantly fell in love with it even more. Lacrosse is the most fun sport I’ve ever played and the brotherhood of being part of a lacrosse team.

watching battle rap – I ventured into the world of battle rapping when I was a junior in High School. A friend of mine shared a video of a rap battle and ever since then I’ve been heavily submerged into the world of Battle rap. The qualities that grasped me to the sport of battle rap is the physicality, the invasion of privacy and imaginative ways of combining words to breakdown an opponent.
